What is a mala necklace?

Traditionally a mala necklace is a prayer necklace used by Yogis during meditation. It is a strand of 109 beads: 108 beads and 1 guru bead. The guru bead serves as a start/end point of a meditation while the other 108 serve as counters during the 108 traditionally silent repetitions of a mantra.
