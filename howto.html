How to use mind.drops

There are two ways to use mind.drops: either use selected affirmations from us via the “Feed my mind” button, or work with your own affirmation via the “I feed my mind” button.

Getting started:

  • Find a quiet place and comfortable place for the next 5-10 minutes.
  • Sit upright and comfortably.
  • Centre yourself by taking 3 deep in- and exhales.
  • Click the “Feed my mind” button to generate an affirmation. / Click the “I feed my mind” button when you like to work with your own affirmation.
  • Read the affirmation / Silently say your own affirmation.
  • The screen will show a flower bud, your starting guru bead.
  • On every further inhale repeat the affirmation.
  • At the end of your exhale tap the screen to move a bead.
  • A mind.drop session ends after 108 repetitions of the affirmation. The screen will show a blossom, simply use the x to close the session. If you turned on sound a gong will remind you of the completion too, hence feel free to do a session with your eyes closed.
  • Smile and let the planted see grow within you.
